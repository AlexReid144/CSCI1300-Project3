******************** WELCOLME TO DUNGEON ESCAPE ********************
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Are you ready to embark on a mythical journey where you and a group of 
players attempt to escape the dungeon where you have been imprisoned.
You will encounter many hardships but you if you work as a team and test your 
luck you can defeat the sorcerer and escape from captivity.
The trip may be grueling, but you can use your wits to find a way to make it out but beware:
YOU MIGHT NOT ALL MAKE IT!!!
